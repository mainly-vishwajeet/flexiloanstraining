## üè¶ RBI & Banking Fundamentals

### RBI & Money Concept

**Key Principle:** All money belongs to RBI (Reserve Bank of India)

- Money is **NOT a product** and cannot be sold
- Money can only be **transacted**
- Money can be **lent and repaid**
- Selling money is **NOT ALLOWED** as per RBI rules

### BFSI Framework

Money transactions are allowed only in these areas under RBI:

1. **Banking** - All banks
2. **Financial Services** - NBFCs (Flexi Loans, Muthoot, etc.)
3. **Services Sector**
   - Schools
   - Colleges
   - Hotels
   - Restaurants
4. **Investments/Insurance**

### IT (Income Tax)
- IT department monitors and controls all financial activities

### NPCI (National Payments Corporation of India)
Manages banking transactions:
- UPI
- Net Banking
- Other digital banking transactions

### ECS (Earlier System) - Legacy
- NBFCs used **physical cheques** for loan distribution
- Cheque contained:
  - EMI amount
  - EMI date
  - Transfer authorization
  - Customer signature
- **Rule:** If loan has 24 EMIs but customer has only 12 cheques ‚Üí NOT ELIGIBLE

### NACH (Current System)
Modern e-mandate system that replaced ECS:
- Works through **E-mandate** and **E-sign**
- EMI amount **automatically debited** from customer's bank account
- No physical cheque required

### Business Account Types

In Flexi Loans, customer distribution:
- **Current Account (CA):** 75% of applicants
- **Overdraft (OD):** 10% of applicants
- **Cash Credit (CC):** 15% of applicants

**Important Rule:** If customer has both Savings and Current Account:
- Ask which account has more transactions
- If Savings has more transactions ‚Üí Advise customer to generate more transactions in Current Account for 6 months and contact again

## üí∞ Loan Products & Features

### Loan Amount
**‚Çπ50,000 to ‚Çπ25,00,000**

### Tenure
**12 to 36 months**

### Interest Rates
**Flat Interest Rate:** 1% - 2% per month (12% - 24% per annum)

### Types of Rate of Interest (ROI)

#### 1. Flat ROI
- EMI remains constant every month
- Example:
  - Principal: ‚Çπ5,000
  - Interest: ‚Çπ1,600
  - Same amount every month

#### 2. Floating ROI
- Interest may increase or decrease based on market conditions

#### 3. Reducing ROI
- Interest decreases as principal reduces
- Example:
  - EMI 1: Principal ‚Çπ5,000 + Interest ‚Çπ1,600
  - EMI 2: Principal ‚Çπ5,100 + Interest ‚Çπ1,500

### Loan Type
- **Term Loan**
- **Unsecured Business Loan**
- Also called **SME / MSME Loan**

### Key Features
‚úÖ 100% Digital Process  
‚úÖ No Collateral Required  
‚úÖ No EMI Locking  
‚úÖ No Usage Restrictions  
‚úÖ Quick Disbursal (24-48 hours)  
‚úÖ Foreclosure/Pre-closure Available  

### EMI Calculation Example

**Formula:** (Principal + Interest Amount) √∑ Tenure

**Example:**
- Principal: ‚Çπ1,00,000
- Interest rate: 20%
- Interest amount: ‚Çπ20,000
- Total payable: ‚Çπ1,20,000
- Tenure: 24 months

**EMI = (‚Çπ1,00,000 + ‚Çπ20,000) √∑ 24 = ‚Çπ5,000**

### Processing Charges
- **Processing Fee:** 2.5% of loan amount
- **GST on Processing Fee:** 18%
- These charges are **deducted from principal** before disbursal
- Remaining amount is credited to customer's account

### Foreclosure / Pre-closure
If customer pays the **entire outstanding loan amount at once** before tenure completion:
- Called **Foreclosure** or **Pre-closure**

### Gold Loan Rule
- Only **ONE active gold loan** is eligible
- More than one gold loan ‚Üí Loan NOT approved

## üìä CIBIL & Credit Rules

### CIBIL Score Range

| Score Range | Category | Status |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| **-1** | NTC (New to CIBIL) | Special Policy |
| **0 - 300** | Bad | Very Poor |
| **300 - 500** | Average | Below Average |
| **500 - 700** | Good | Good |
| **700 - 900** | Excellent | Excellent |

**Important:** CIBIL and related data updates every **45 days**

### Loan Rejection Engines

Two main engines cause loan rejection:

#### 1. BRE (Banking Rule Engine)
Checks:
- **BTO (Monthly Transactions)**
  - ‚Çπ2 lakh or more ‚Üí Eligible
  - ‚Çπ1 lakh to ‚Çπ1.5 lakh ‚Üí Review needed
  - Less than ‚Çπ1 lakh ‚Üí Rejected

- **ABB (Average Bank Balance)**
  - Range: ‚Çπ15,000 - ‚Çπ45,000
  - Based on last 6 months bank statement
  - Minimum 3+ new customer transactions per month required

- **EMI Date Check**
  - Common dates: 1, 5, 16, 25
  - If ABB falls below 50% on these dates ‚Üí Rejected

#### 2. CRE (Credit Rule Engine)
Checks for rejection reasons:

**1. Cheque / EMI Bounce**
- Monthly limit: Not more than 2
- Yearly limit: Not more than 20

**2. Bounce Charges**
- Varies by bank: ‚Çπ250 / ‚Çπ400 / ‚Çπ500
- Maximum: ‚Çπ750
- Charged once per month
- Penalty after bounce: ‚Çπ100 - ‚Çπ150 per day
- **Overdue Calculation:** Bounce charge + penalty = Overdue
  - Overdue ‚â• ‚Çπ1000 ‚Üí Must be cleared first
  - Overdue < ‚Çπ1000 ‚Üí Acceptable

**3. Due Past Date**
- Example: EMI date is 5th
- If EMI bounces and is NOT paid within 10 days
- Due past date > 10 days ‚Üí ‚ùå REJECTED

**4. Enquiry Limit**
- Per Month: Less than 3 enquiries
- Per Year: Less than 12 enquiries
- Enquiry means: CIBIL check or loan application
