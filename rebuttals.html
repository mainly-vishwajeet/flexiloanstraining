Rebuttals

Common customer objections and professional rebuttals in English & Hindi

Not Interested / No Time

English

Customer says:

"Not interested / No time / Call me later"

Rebuttal:

"No worries sir/madam, I understand. Just a quick heads-up: this is a completely unsecured business loan up to ₹25 lakhs. If you ever need fast funds without collateral, we can disburse within 24–48 hours. Can I share a quick checklist so you have it handy?"

Tip:

Mark follow-up date, and WhatsApp the brochure if allowed.

Hindi

Customer kehta hai:

"Interested nahi hai / Time nahi hai / Baad mein call karo"

Rebuttal:

"Koi baat nahi sir/madam, main samajh sakta hun. Bas ek quick heads-up: yeh completely unsecured business loan hai ₹25 lakh tak. Agar kabhi aapko bina collateral ke fast funds chahiye, toh hum 24-48 hours mein disburse kar sakte hain. Kya main ek quick checklist share kar sakta hun taaki aapke paas ready rahe?"

Tip:

Follow-up date mark karo, aur agar allowed hai toh WhatsApp par brochure bhejo.

Privacy Concerns

English

Customer says:

"Why should I share turnover/CIBIL/documents?"

Rebuttal:

"That's a fair question! We ask just the minimum details to check if you're eligible — so we don't waste your time. In fact, even a ₹2 lakh monthly turnover and CIBIL of 700 is enough in many cases. Shall I just check and update you?"

Tip:

Teach agents to assure privacy, avoid forcing, and use benchmarks (₹2L MTO, 700+ CIBIL).

Hindi

Customer kehta hai:

"Main kyun share karun turnover/CIBIL/documents?"

Rebuttal:

"Yeh bilkul fair question hai! Hum sirf minimum details maangte hain taaki check kar sakein ki aap eligible hain ya nahi — taaki aapka time waste na ho. Actually, sirf ₹2 lakh monthly turnover aur 700 CIBIL bhi kaafi cases mein enough hai. Kya main bas check kar ke aapko update kar dun?"

Tip:

Agents ko sikhao privacy assure karna, force nahi karna, aur benchmarks use karna (₹2L MTO, 700+ CIBIL).

Bank Statement Hesitation

English

Customer says:

"I'm not comfortable sharing my bank statement"

Rebuttal:

"Totally understand. Just to clarify, we only check your cash flow, we don't access any private info. It's required to match the ₹2 lakh turnover and ₹15K balances on a few key dates — which helps us give you the best offer."

Tip:

We accept PDF bank statements or online fetch with consent.

Hindi

Customer kehta hai:

"Main comfortable nahi hun apna bank statement share karne mein"

Rebuttal:

"Bilkul samajh sakta hun. Bas clarify karna chahta hun, hum sirf aapka cash flow check karte hain, koi private info access nahi karte. Yeh ₹2 lakh turnover aur kuch key dates par ₹15K balance match karne ke liye required hai — jo humein aapko best offer dene mein help karta hai."

Tip:

Hum PDF bank statements accept karte hain ya consent ke saath online fetch kar sakte hain.

Document Delay

English

Customer says:

"I don't want to give documents now"

Rebuttal:

"No problem! We can first check your basic eligibility with just your PAN and business pin code. If it looks good, you can share documents only when you're ready to proceed."

Tip:

Break the process into 2 steps – PAN + Pincode first → Docs later.

Hindi

Customer kehta hai:

"Main abhi documents nahi dena chahta"

Rebuttal:

"Koi problem nahi! Hum pehle sirf aapka PAN aur business pin code se basic eligibility check kar sakte hain. Agar accha lagta hai, toh documents sirf tab share kar sakte hain jab aap proceed karne ke liye ready hon."

Tip:

Process ko 2 steps mein break karo – pehle PAN + Pincode → baad mein Docs.

Existing Loan

English

Customer says:

"I've taken loan from someone else"

Rebuttal:

"That's great! That means you have repayment history. We also offer top-up loans after 1/3rd tenure and give you the flexibility to close early without part-payment charges. Would you like to compare the ROI or terms?"

Tip:

Use comparison pitch – ROI (1–2%), fast disbursal, flexible terms.

Hindi

Customer kehta hai:

"Maine kisi aur se loan le liya hai"

Rebuttal:

"Yeh toh great hai! Matlab aapka repayment history hai. Hum 1/3rd tenure ke baad top-up loans bhi offer karte hain aur aapko flexibility dete hain ki bina part-payment charges ke early close kar sakte hain. Kya aap ROI ya terms compare karna chahenge?"

Tip:

Comparison pitch use karo – ROI (1–2%), fast disbursal, flexible terms.

Suspicious About Call

English

Customer says:

"What's the catch? Why are you calling me?"

Rebuttal:

"I'm calling you on behalf of FlexiLoans, one of India's leading digital lenders. You may have shown interest through a partner site or business portal. We're just offering you a chance to check if you're eligible for fast, unsecured funds."

Tip:

Keep partner sources handy (like India MART, Lending kart leads, etc.)

Hindi

Customer kehta hai:

"Kya catch hai? Aap mujhe kyun call kar rahe hain?"

Rebuttal:

"Main aapko FlexiLoans ki taraf se call kar raha hun, jo India ke leading digital lenders mein se ek hai. Aapne shayad kisi partner site ya business portal ke through interest dikhaya hoga. Hum bas aapko chance de rahe hain check karne ka ki aap fast, unsecured funds ke liye eligible hain ya nahi."

Tip:

Partner sources ready rakhiye (jaise India MART, Lending kart leads, etc.)

Document Related Questions

English

Customer says:

"Why do you need my PAN card and address proof with matching details?"

Rebuttal:

"We collect your PAN card and address proof to verify your identity and ensure consistency in your documentation. The name, date of birth, and father's name should match on both documents to avoid any discrepancies during the verification and credit assessment process. This is a standard KYC (Know Your Customer) compliance required by all RBI-regulated lenders."

Hindi

Customer kehta hai:

"Aapko mere PAN card aur address proof matching details ke saath kyun chahiye?"

Rebuttal:

"Hum aapka PAN card aur address proof aapki identity verify karne ke liye aur documentation mein consistency ensure karne ke liye collect karte hain. Name, date of birth, aur father's name dono documents mein match hona chahiye taaki verification aur credit assessment process mein koi discrepancy na ho. Yeh standard KYC (Know Your Customer) compliance hai jo sabhi RBI-regulated lenders ke liye required hai."

English

Customer says:

"Why do you need business KYC and what is it used for?"

Rebuttal:

"Sir/Ma'am, we collect Business KYC documents to confirm the vintage of your business (how old your business is) and to validate the business address. These documents should include your proprietor name, legal business status, full address with pincode, and date of registration. This helps our credit team assess your business credibility and overall loan eligibility. It must be a government-issued document such as Udyam, GST, or Shop Act."

Hindi

Customer kehta hai:

"Aapko business KYC kyun chahiye aur iska use kya hai?"

Rebuttal:

"Sir/Ma'am, hum Business KYC documents aapke business ka vintage confirm karne ke liye collect karte hain (ki aapka business kitna purana hai) aur business address validate karne ke liye. In documents mein aapka proprietor name, legal business status, full address with pincode, aur registration date hona chahiye. Yeh hamari credit team ko aapki business credibility aur overall loan eligibility assess karne mein help karta hai. Yeh government-issued document hona chahiye jaise Udyam, GST, ya Shop Act."

English

Customer says:

"Why do you need my last 6 months' bank statements in a specific format?"

Rebuttal:

"We require your latest 6 months' bank statement in net banking PDF format to analyze your monthly turnover and cash flow. This helps us calculate your loan eligibility. The firm name on your bank statement should match the one in your business KYC to ensure consistency."

We specifically check for:

  • MTO (Monthly Turnover): Minimum ₹2 lakhs to be eligible
  • ABB (Average Bank Balance): ₹15,000 on 1st, 5th, 16th, and 25th
  • Bank logo: Ensures the document is authentic
  • The credit column is used to track the revenue flow of your business

Clear banking helps us offer you the maximum eligible amount with the best rate of interest.

Hindi

Customer kehta hai:

"Aapko mere last 6 months ke bank statements specific format mein kyun chahiye?"

Rebuttal:

"Humein aapka latest 6 months ka bank statement net banking PDF format mein chahiye taaki hum aapka monthly turnover aur cash flow analyze kar sakein. Yeh humein aapki loan eligibility calculate karne mein help karta hai. Aapke bank statement par firm name aapke business KYC se match hona chahiye consistency ensure karne ke liye."

Hum specifically yeh check karte hain:

  • MTO (Monthly Turnover): Eligible hone ke liye minimum ₹2 lakh
  • ABB (Average Bank Balance): 1st, 5th, 16th, aur 25th ko ₹15,000
  • Bank logo: Document authentic hai ya nahi ensure karta hai
  • Credit column aapke business ke revenue flow track karne ke liye use hota hai

Clear banking humein aapko maximum eligible amount best rate of interest ke saath offer karne mein help karta hai.
