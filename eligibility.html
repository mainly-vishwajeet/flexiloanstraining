"As per internal policy, we cannot approve your application right now."

ABB (Average Bank Balance)

Acceptable Range

₹15,000 to ₹45,000

Check Period

Last 6 months bank statement

Requirement

Minimum 3 or more new customer transactions per month

EMI Date Check

Common EMI Dates

1 5 16 25

Critical Rule

  • Based on last 6 months statements
  • Check ABB on common EMI dates (1, 5, 16, 25)
  • If ABB falls below 50% on these dates → Case REJECTED

Example:

Customer's ABB: ₹30,000

50% of ABB: ₹15,000

Check: If balance on EMI dates (1, 5, 16, 25) is below ₹15,000 → REJECTED

Gold Loan Rule

Critical Rule

Allowed

Only ONE active gold loan is eligible

Not Allowed

More than one gold loan → Loan NOT approved

Verification Process:

  • Check CIBIL report for gold loan history
  • Verify with customer's bank statements
  • Confirm closure of previous gold loans if any
