Risk Assessment Guidelines

Key Factors to Consider:

Market Volatility

How much the business is affected by market fluctuations

Regulatory Risk

Possibility of business being banned or heavily regulated

Customer Base

Stability and diversity of customer base

Cash Flow Predictability

How predictable and stable the business income is

Inventory Risk

Risk of inventory becoming obsolete or losing value

Dependency Risk

Over-dependence on specific suppliers or customers

Assessment Process:

1

Identify Business Type

Clearly categorize the exact nature of business

2

Check Sub-categories

Drill down to specific sub-types within the category

3

Assess Risk Factors

Evaluate all relevant risk factors for the business

4

Assign Risk Color

Categorize as Green, Orange, Blue, or Red based on assessment

5

Apply Policy

Use appropriate policy based on risk categorization
